Anxiety Opponents

With this technique you can let your fear opponent shrink, so that he loses his threatening effect.

An "opponent of fear" is a person in whose presence one feels inhibited and / or insecure. With the following procedure you can quickly and effectively eliminate such discomfort.


  1. Think of a person in whose presence you feel so far fearful, insecure, inhibited, nervous and similar. Where and how do you see the image in front of your "spiritual eye"? Is it further away or rather in front of your nose? Does the person in your imagination have the real size or does it seem bigger? Look straight ahead, rather down or more upwards when you look at the person? Is the image of the person more left or more right? Is the image colored or black-and-white? What feeling arises in you when you think of this person? Where in your body do you feel this feeling most strongly? If you don't consciously see your inner images, you still process them on an unconscious level. Just imagine in this case, you would be able to see the images consciously.
  2. Now imagine a small black and white TV, a few meters away from you and on the bottom left.
  3. See the image of this person now in the small black and white TV. What changes in your feeling when you see this person as a small black and white image, at the bottom left of the floor? (In general, the discomfort decreases significantly, or even dissolves completely.)
  4. Now, with your eyes closed, let the image of the person reappear in the original way in front of your spiritual eye (e.g. in the upper part of the field of vision, larger than real). Then let the image of the person shrink, and see it as in the small black and white TV, a few meters away from you at the bottom left.
  5. Repeat step 4 five to eight times. It is important that you close your eyes at the beginning and open them for a few seconds at the end before you go to the next repetition. The repetitions train your brain to perform this process automatically in the future. The former "overpowerful" fear opponent shrinks back in your experience to the normal person he is. And you can face this person again in the future with all the power of your abilities.
  6. Now imagine that you would meet this person. How do you experience the situation now?

If you are not satisfied with the result, repeat step 4 again. Sometimes more frequent repetitions are necessary to achieve the desired result.
