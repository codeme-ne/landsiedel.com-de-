NLP Practitioner training

In the NLP Practitioner training, you will learn and apply the basics of neurolinguistic programming. This NLP training is excellent for people who want to develop both privately and professionally. Most of the participants use the NLP training as a NLP Practitioner mainly to do something for themselves and their personality development. Many develop so much enthusiasm that they will later consider whether they even want to become coach themselves. NLP can be used both privately and professionally excellently. In any case, you will develop your personality very strongly. Our NLP Practitioner trainings are carried out by NLP Master Trainers of the International Association of NLP Institutes. This is the world's largest NLP association. Stephan Landsiedel is President of the Association for Germany. He has already conducted over 4,000 NLP training days and personally trained a large part of the active German NLP trainers.

Subjects and topics of training

Perception
A sharpened perception is a prerequisite for successful communication. Only those who see what triggers their words and body language can react accordingly and act in accordance with their needs. Therefore, at the beginning of this training program there are a series of perceptual exercises, which are further trained during the further seminar blocks.
Keywords: Training of one's own perception during communication with others, increased attention to body language and vocal qualities, differences between interpretation and sense-specific language, concept of calibration, targeted observation and use of body language, giving and receiving feedback


Pacing and Leading
Master of communication meets a conversational partner in his experience of the world and is able to guide him on this basis. Learn art through verbal and nonverbal communication to establish deep trusting contact and to conduct the conversation.
Tags: Repeat production through the concept of mirroring, pacing of mood, pacing of statements, pacing of body language, taking over the leadership, interrupting the rapport, active listening


Enable resources
Our emotional states determine our lives. The ability to adapt your emotional states to the needs of a situation helps you not only to achieve more success but also to enjoy life. Moreover, if you have the ability to change the emotional states of other people, you will be happy to visit customers, employees and partners.
Key words: Use existing resources to put the interlocutor and his/her team into positive conditions – build up a positive group atmosphere and make it available at any time – change of his/her own physiology as an elegant method to change his/her own states – dissolution of depression, blockages and stucco states in customer and employee discussions – implementation of the So-Tun-as-Ob principle – development of power over the body


Anchors
Facilitate everyday life with the knowledge and the ability to create our natural conditioning mechanisms. Learn to create beneficial emotional reactions and dissolve restrictive ones whenever you want.
Keywords: anchoring: theory, definition, procedure, special features, identification of existing anchors, anchor set, negative anchor disempowered, anchor chain and stack, use of hidden anchors in sales and employee talks, exercise moment of excellence, anchor for speakers and sellers


Metamodel of language
Be aware of the magic of your language. It is not only the basis for clear communication, but also for fast, targeted change. Use this NLP technique to become one of the leading language tools of our time. Become a master of spoken language.
Tags: Four simple facts about our perception, perception filters, basic information processing processes, recovery of lost information, transformation model: crash course, language pattern of the meta model, eradication, generalization, distortion, hearing and self-constructing application examples, what does happiness mean?, problem and destination determination, wish staircase, Future Pace, Future Pace on sale


Representation system
Immerse yourself in the sense-specific world of your interlocutors in order to gain deeper understanding and understanding. This sensual consciousness opens up unimagined levels of communication.
Key words: Introduction to a representation system that uses different senses, language and sensory system, the knowledge of the representation system to deepen the relationship – using minimal hints (e.g. breathing, speaking speed, tonality) to recognize how the customer or employee processes information and finds access to his internal resources, introduction to mini-strategies, eye access instructions: explanation and application e.g. in sales interview, sales strategies for VAK customers (role game), history and basics of the NLP


Hypnotic language patterns
Open the door to you unconscious potential by knowing and applying the Milton model of language. Hypnotic language patterns allow you access to trance states, and thus to levels of consciousness that are otherwise closed to you.
Tags: prejudices and objections to hypnosis, Why learn hypnosis?, concept clarifications hypnosis, trance, history of hypnosis, goals of Erickson, hypnosis principles, first exercises, language patterns of the Milton model, hints for trance inductions and ending a trance, construction of metaphors, metaphors, exercises


Reframing
The ability to illuminate a behavior or a situation from different perspectives makes our mind free and mobile. Train the ability to see meaning flexibly and thereby increase the possibilities of choice considerably.
Keywords: definition of reframing, types of reframing: context and meaning reframing, practice examples, joke trance, separation of intention and behavior, part model in NLP, parts party, six-step refracting, negotiation between parts, objection treatment by reframing, agrrement frame


Sub-modalities:
Discover the programming language of subjective experience with this groundbreaking NLP technique. This high-profile tool allows you to quickly and purposefully influence your emotional life.
Tags: verbal indicators of submodalities (SM), checklist of possible SM, exercises to the SM, working out critical SM, contrast method, sending resources, destruction of inner images and sounds, praline pattern: building a strong motivation for unloved things, changing beliefs, fear opponents, swish technique, phobia technique


Strategies
Behind human behavior are strategies that control and control it. In the NLP, methods have been developed that allow you to learn and teach the strategies of experts (e.g., Walt Disney's creativity strategy). In this way, for example, creativity, flexibility, motivation and decision-making processes can be optimized.
Tags: What are strategies?, Walt Disney strategy, The TOTE model, NLP notation, Eliciting a strategy, Conditions of well-formed strategies, Spelling strategy, Flexibility strategy, Love strategy, Utilization of strategies, Design of a strategy, Streamlining of strategies, Redesign of a strategy, Installing a strategy, Interrupting strategies, Application possibilities


Perception positions
Often we are trapped in our own view of things. Learn to become flexible, to look at yourself from the outside and thereby gain completely new possibilities to deal with stress situations.
Keywords: perception positions, position 1-2-3, interference of perception positions, neurological planes, determination of targets by the neurological planes


Life-cycle work
Use your timeline to make systematic planning of your goals and gain clarity in integrating your personal experiences in the past for your future.
Keywords: basics of timeline work, eliciting the timeline, different types of timeline, spatial configuration of the timeline, language patterns, expressions, submodalities of time, removal of bumps and holes, design criteria, tempting future, time-line refracting, re-imprinting


Well-formed objectives
Define your goals clearly and well shaped according to the NLP criteria for goals. By doing so, realize more and more the life you really want.

Overview of training plan

The NLP Practitioner Training is the first right level in NLP training. For this purpose, taster seminars such as NLP base day, experience weekend or introductory webinars can be attended. In any case, the Practitioner Training starts from the very beginning and can therefore be attended without prior knowledge. At the end of the training, you master numerous NLP formats and can apply them in different contexts.
If you want to expand your skills and skills after the practitioner, you will find another very extensive training in the NLP master's training. This is then about learning more formats and, above all, becoming even more experienced in applying them in the various everyday and professional situations.

What does the NLP trainee training bring me?

The NLP Practitioner course is the first of three stages in the training of the NLP. In the Practitioner course the basic theoretical knowledge and practical skills about the methods are taught.

Advantages of the Practitioner:

  • In training as an NLP practitioner, you learn to dramatically improve communication with yourself and other people and how you can develop and use your potential.
  • You will be taught and taught learning and creativity strategies that will help you develop further in these areas.
  • Changing to energetic and self-confident states and overcoming blockades are also among the training topics.
  • NLP offers a variety of methods to make oneself aware of his feelings, as well as behavioural and thinking patterns.
  • NLP also offers many ways to Personal strengths and skills to discover and develop.

The goal is to teach you methods and strategies with which you can increase your quality of life.
NLP is used in almost every area of life – leadership, therapy, sports, health, rhetoric, partnership, education, sales.

However, the NLP practitioner is not intended as a replacement therapy.
