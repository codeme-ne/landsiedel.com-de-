NLP Master's training

The NLP-Master-Practitioner builds on the NLP-Practitioner content. The focus is on the integrative and situation-specific application of the learned formats. Prerequisite for the certification of the training is a completed NLP-Practitioner-Training. Our NLP-Master-Trainings are led by highly qualified and long-term NLP-Master-Trainers. We offer the Master both in several presence- as well as in several online formats according to the high international quality standards of the International Association of NLP-Institutes.




Prerequisites for Master's training

Prerequisite for a later certification of the NLP-Master-Training according to the guidelines of the International Association of NLP-Institutes is a completed NLP-Practitioner-Training according to the guidelines of this association. This means that the participant submits a copy of the Practitioner-certificate, which is tested by us on the minimum requirements. If the applicant can show only the certification by another NLP-Verband, there is the possibility of a post-certification (cost approx. 100 Euro for seal fees) if the training has met the minimum standards. Two important standards are that the training took at least 18 days and at least 130 hours (passes are counted out). Another criterion is that the standard training contents of a practitioner have been covered.

If the minimum standards have only been barely met, there may be a possibility to make up for the missing days, hours and content for a fee.
However, if the Practitioner Training took only 10 days or less, more than half of the content is simply missing. If you are looking for certification in this case, we strongly recommend that you complete a correspondingly qualified NLP Practitioner Training. In practice, too large gaps have been shown here, which then significantly lower the level of Master Training.

Some participants are not interested in certification. They would like to participate in the training without certification. This is possible if the participant assures us that he has sufficient NLP-practitioner-knowledge and skills even without formal certification. There are sometimes cases, e.g. if the NLP-training is so long ago that there were no associations yet and in the meantime regularly worked with clients ... or the skills come from other trainings, but have not been certified etc.

Please understand that we adhere to the quality specifications of the association, in whose name we assign the seals and create certificates. Our service team will be pleased to inform you which solutions are available in your case.



General information on Master's training

Overview NLP Master Training

In addition to learning many newer NLP formats, the NLP master practitioner is primarily concerned with the ability to use the techniques from the practitioner in a goal-oriented and independent manner. Thus, it is practiced here to see solutions for predetermined problems on the customer side with the help of the already known tools.

In addition, the NLP Master practitioner has a very extensive part on the subject of life design. The participants are supposed to shape their own life. Important key topics in the NLP Master practitioner are: values, beliefs and their own vision.

The NLP Master Practitioner builds on the NLP Practitioner content. The focus is on the integrative and situation-specific application of the learned formats. Some new elements complete the program. A Master Practitioner has worked intensively with NLP and uses NLP as a tool to shape his own life. He pays attention to his highest values and develops his personality in an ecological way.

Historical development

In the history of the NLP, the NLP-practitioner first developed the NLP-practitioner-training before the NLP-Master-practitioner. "Practitioner" means something like "practitioner, practitioner". In these training programs a lot was practiced and trained. After the completion of this training program the participants could apply the basic techniques of the NLP. Over time more and more NLP techniques were developed. The training extended to over 40 days and thus the new techniques began to be transferred to a second course. This new training program was called NLP-Master-practitioner. Often, however, NLP-Master-practitioner is shortened with NLP-Master. After successful completion of the NLP-Master-practitioner, the participants know all the essential NLP techniques and receive the Master-certificate from the International Association of NLP-Institutes (IN).

Testing modalities and master thesis

In a test, both theoretical and practical knowledge is checked. In addition, the acquisition of the Master-Practitioner-certificate requires the independent processing of a small topic. The topic can be chosen freely or at the suggestion of the trainer.
The Master-Practitioner-Certificate entitles to participate in the NLP-Trainer-Training.

Rules of representation

In most of our NLP trainings (except for the online evening variant) there is a main trainer responsible for certification and the process. Depending on the trainer and location, another NLP trainer may take over the training for four to a maximum of five days. If a trainer is prevented due to an emergency situation, we hire a replacement trainer or offer a replacement appointment.

Subjects and topics of training

Meta-programs
Meta programs are person-specific perception filters that become visible in the behavior of a person. They edit, shape and design those information from the outside world that we allow to get inside. Learn to recognize and use these programs with you and others. By doing so, you expand your ability to assess other people and make reports.


Sleight of Mouth-Pattern
This is more than just a training in punchiness. With the help of short, pointed phrases, you can reframe restrictive beliefs powerfully. The Sleight of Mouth patterns can be used specifically in everyday life. Often, no special settings are required for this. You learn very concrete possibilities of reframing.


Modeling
Learn how to reproduce human excellence at an advanced level. This block closes the circle of NLP beginnings to today's applications. Learn to find out the patterns of successful people and make them usable for yourself and others.


Working with beliefs
Success blockades are only too often in ourselves. Sometimes they are completely unconscious to us, but often they also appear in the form of sentences that go through our minds when we start a project or get a job. They are beliefs like: "I'm too young/old. I don't have money. I don't have enough education/experience. I don't have good relationships. I'm from simple relationships." etc. Our beliefs form the backbone of our reality constructs. Learn in this block what influence these sentences have on your life, how they originated and how you can change them with you and others. Train yourself to create more useful beliefs.


Working with values and lifestyles
Values such as freedom, love, honesty or success are our inner motivators. They ultimately determine and guide our actions. Because of them, wars are waged, terrible torments are endured and people are destroyed. You learn in this block how values arise, how you can find out your personal hierarchy of values. You deal with your conflicts of values and rules of life and you train to get out in a conversation the values and rules of life of your partner or your colleague. If you act in accordance with your values, then you will feel a new strong energy in you.


Core transformation
In the Core Transformation process, the behaviors, feelings and reactions that you least like about yourself are identified. Then, they are used to embark on an amazing healing and uplifting inner journey into the depths of being with their help. In other words, this method allows us to achieve conditions that allow other people to sit and meditate on mountains for years.


Diamond technology
This technique is suitable for therapeutic assistance, coaching as well as for consulting situations and everyday life. This technique not only solves problems, but also resolves them in a literal sense. This is achieved by changing the background construction itself, in which the problem and the respective solution were embedded so far. The result is often surprisingly fast changes that are both profound and sustainable.


Spiral Dynamics / Graves Levels
This model represents a far-reaching theory about human development. Our different worldviews, our beliefs, even our identity are represented by eight "memes" or value systems that can be applied to individuals as well as to whole cultures. Get to know the different value systems and use them for your personal development or even in systemic contexts.


Advanced hypnosis techniques
Advanced methods of hypnosis are trained. Refine your skills in trance induction. Get to know some exciting trance phenomena and suggestibility tests. Use trance elements in everyday communication processes, e.g. in sales and leadership situations or in dealing with partners and family. The possibilities and uses of self hypnosis are also discussed.

Frequently asked questions about training (FAQ)

Can I attend the NLP Master training without any previous NLP knowledge?
No, it doesn't make any sense without prior knowledge.
But a visit without fully completed and certified NLP practitioner training is possible.
However, we will not be able to issue you a certificate, since the association requires a completed NLP trainee training. This also applies if you pass the testing - unfortunately. This is a rule that we have not made. We adhere to the specifications, which are also useful for the majority of participants. Sometimes, however, there are exceptions, so we do not want to rule out participation in principle.
In many places, an NLP pre-knowledge is really necessary. The trainers are not able to go back to all the many details of the trainee training during the master's training. Often, the co-trainers are ready to summarise the essentials again during the break, but previous knowledge is definitely important - even if it is not a certified training, but the knowledge and skills has been acquired elsewhere.

I have completed my NLP internship training in another institute, can I complete my master's degree at Landsiedel?
In order to complete the NLP Master training, you will need a completed NLP Practitioner training. This does not have to be completed with us. For this we need a readable copy (incl. seal) of your certificate of the NLP Practitioner training after your registration. You can send the copy to us by e-mail.

What kind of certificate do I get with my degree?
With successful completion you will receive the certification according to LNLPT (Landsiedel NLP Training) as well as the International Association of NLP Institutes (IN).

Is there a testing/examination for the Master's training?
Yes, both theoretical and practical knowledge are tested in a test. In addition, a master's thesis is required in the master's thesis.

What training can I attend after the NLP Master training?
After the NLP-Master-Training you can participate in the NLP-Trainer-Training or the NLP-Coach-Training (with completion of the Master-Coach).

How can I get to know the trainer in advance?
Use the free webinars, videos or participate in an NLP base tag.

What is included in the price?
The price includes the seminar days, the welcome folder, seminar documents and the certificate. Each participant takes care of arrival, meals and, if necessary, overnight stay. We charge no costs for this from our side. So there is no obligatory conference fee or something similar.

Where can I stay nearby? Where can I park?
We have seminar descriptions for all our locations, in which overnight stays and parking facilities are shown as download/PDF. In Kitzingen there are some parking places directly at the villa. Participants who stay overnight will usually find a parking space at their hotel alternatively we recommend public parking in a few minutes away.

Can I pay in installments?
We do not offer installment payment. However, you can contact a third party.

Does the booking date apply to the early booking price or the payment receipt?
The early booking price depends on the booking date, if the registration is received before the deadline, you will receive the favorable early booking price.

When is the payment due?
The payment is due 28 days after the invoice date by bank transfer to the account of Landsiedel NLP Training. If the time between the registration and the start of the seminar is less than 28 days, the full amount is due immediately. The cash receipt is decisive in our account. In the case of short-term registrations, Landsiedel NLP Training can request payment in cash on the event day. If the period between the registration and the start of the event is more than 6 months, the payment is due only 6 months before the start of the event. The participant receives an invoice or cash payment a payment receipt.
