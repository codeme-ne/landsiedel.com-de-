NLP Here, NLP because, you hear so much everywhere about how effective NLP is supposed to be and what it supposedly does to other people...


  • But is there any evidence?
  • Any practice-tested references?
  • Examples from people who have experienced it firsthand, something to identify with?

First of all: In the NLP, certain Techniques, with the help of which every situation in which you are currently in can be mastered. These techniques are already very old and tested, so for example, participants of our coaching or students can report on one of our trainings, about groundbreaking results they have learned from one of the best coaches in Germany through NLP!
– Stephan Landsiedel

Although NLP trainings as well as corresponding practice groups (so-called peer groups) and practice evenings offer a wealth of possibilities to apply, practice and try out what you have learned. But is this really enough to be able to open up the whole "magic" of the NLP for yourself and apply it in a wide variety of situations?

Maybe you're wondering where you can use NLP anywhere...

In short, wherever people communicate with each other or with themselves. (When I first thought about this question, I was amazed at how many possibilities there are.)



Paper is patient – it's said – and that's what a lot of "gurus" make use of, because you can say a lot, but what about results? So that you can convince yourself of what NLP can do for people, - if it has been properly taught and practiced!- Some of our customers shared their successes with us:

(The following NLP examples are people of different ages and professional backgrounds. However, they have one thing in common: They use NLP in their everyday lives and thus shape their lives a bit more successfully.)



Markus (32) Head of a gym

Most people come to us because they are not satisfied with their body and want to change it. Often it happens that after about three months the first euphoria is over, people stop training and are also frustrated because they have "failed". I have made it my task to prevent that! I am convinced: If you believe in yourself and have the right attitude, you can achieve everything! That's why I am in close contact with our members – and this helps me NLP daily!

I personally attended an NLP seminar on Target work the experience of how good it feels, If you deal with your goals, then tackle them and finally reach them! And I can now pass this knowledge on to my members. I understand that every person has something very specific that motivates him – that's what I'm talking about with our members, I'm visualizing their dreams with them, and then empowering them to live them.

For example, let's take the famous "dream body": Thanks to NLP, I know how important it is to imagine exactly what my personal dream body looks like! How much do I want to weigh? How many muscles do I want to have? Until exactly when do I want to have achieved this? Best with exact date. – These are things I ask my members, and they always remind them of it – and since I've done this, hardly anyone else jumps off before he has reached his goal! Then they are proud and grateful. And that also gives me a great feeling.

Applied NLP techniques: Time line, Strategies.

T. Knappmeier (43) Office clerk

It is my job to advise customers. Especially with regard to finance, many people are insecure and therefore afraid to do something wrong. It is up to me to take away this uncertainty from them. Communication training from NLP has given me many practical tips for this, It's no use to the customer if I know about the technical aspects and they still don't understand anything. Therefore, it is my task to ask what exactly they imagine, where exactly the ambiguities are, and only by doing so, something is already clear for many customers. And then, of course, it is important that I can explain complex facts to them in such a way that they understand it – and in doing so, I have to be able to adapt myself to my counterpart. Alone the concept of the different ‘maps' has helped me – each person has his own rules, and within each map they have their own rules. What is incredibly important to me, can be of no importance to the customer – in his/her own Map And if I know that, I can accept that and more Focus For me, it is the greatest thing if the customers feel well advised and I know that I have been able to provide them with my knowledge – both from finance and from NLP – competently!
