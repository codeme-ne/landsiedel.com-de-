NLP Library: Reframing & Refraiming

Wrong spelling of Reframing

Reframing... The ability to illuminate a behavior or situation from different perspectives makes our mind free and mobile.

For some time, the false spelling "refraiming" has been in circulation. The term "reframing" derives from the English word "frame", which means as much as "frame".

Reframing literally means giving things a new framework.

Whether the additional "i" is a typing error, or the correct writing is not known, remains obscure. In many documents on the web, this false spelling can already be found.

Extraordinary information about reframing, as well as more free information about NLP finden sie in unserer NLP Bibliothek zu Reframing.
