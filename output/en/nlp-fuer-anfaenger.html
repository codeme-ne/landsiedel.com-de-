What does NLP mean?

NLP is a language and change model with which Techniques you are positive, self-determined and more successful can design. You can compare NLP with a tool box, which is why it is also as ‘dangerous’ because as with a tool box, the Neuro Linguistic Program (short: NLP) includes several tools (techniques), such as a hammer or a knife. These tools can be used either to improve a condition or they can also be abused for other purposes. You alone decide how to use NLP for you when you control it. However, the fact is that no matter how you use it, it remains a powerful psychological tool.

5 powerful NLP techniques

NLP offers effective methods in a wide range of applications. They serve to improve communication and the respective areas of life. All techniques are basically effective, you just have to know how to implement them. Turn Techniques im Alltag an und verbessere deine Wahrnehmung.

  1. Rapport

    Have you ever had a conversation with a person you felt great? You felt understood and you were on a wavelength. When this happens, you speak of Rapport (Rapport = equality). You can create a repeat between you and your interlocutor, so To create sympathy and trust. By the way, it is also possible, among other things, to increase the readiness to buy or to make a price reduction easier.

    But not only in the sale is the ability to produce rapport to its counterpart very important, but also in communication, in discussions and conflict resolutions You're the fastest at your goal when you first build a report.

    In order to successfully produce repeats to another person, two other NLP methods are required: ‘Pacing’ and ‘Leading’.

    Read on here to find out!

  2. Anchors

    The term ‘core’ one understands a stimulus (trigger, stimulus), which triggers a certain, always the same reaction in a person. This means that in some situations we are unconsciously anchoring by combining a feeling (fröhlich/traurig, anxious/mutual, etc.) with a situation. Both positive and negative feelings can be anchored. In order not to be the victim of negative associations, we can use the powerful NLP technique of anchoring for us. How exactly this goes, you can here read more.

    Practice: Put yourself in a situation where you laughed a lot. Take the feeling of pleasure and set an anchor just before the climax of your feeling – for example, ball your hand to a fist and press three times. Turn off and test the anchor again by pressing your handballs back together. Now you should feel refreshed.

    Read on here to find out!

  3. Representation systems: VAKOG

    Man experiences the world through his sense organs. In the NLP we distinguish five different representation systems:

    • Visuell (see/eye)
    • Auditive (listening/earning)
    • Kinesthetically (feeling/aut)
    • Olfactory (seep/nase)
    • Gustatoric (smooth/ceiling)

    Each person tends to be stronger or weaker to some perceptions, so people can be typified (visual, auditive, kinesthetic, olfactory, gustatory). If you know which type your interlocutor is, you can make even easier and faster reports.

    Read on here to find out!

  4. Swish technique

    With the Swish technique can be dissolving or changing bad habits and strong Building motivation. These are pictures that we project in our head before our spiritual eye.

    Practice: Imagine an image of your unwanted behavior (e.g. nail chews) and create a target image of how you want it instead. Then you exchange both pictures with the help of SWISH-TECHNIK out.

    Read on here to find out!

  5. Meta model

    With Meta model to the communication championship: Repayments, generalizations and distortions are frequently used in speaking, which often misinterprets statements and conflicts arise.

    With the meta-model, you impress with the depth structure of the said, clarify unclearness and avoid misunderstandings.
