NLP Diploma Education – The perfect entry into the world of the NLP

The NLP Diploma training offers a well-founded and easily accessible entry into the world of the NLP. It depends on:

  • Coaches and therapists
  • Managers and educators
  • Private persons interested in personality development

No previous knowledge is required, which means that the training is suitable for those who want to improve their skills in the field of communication and self-management. The aim is to communicate clearly and effectively, define personal goals and implement them sustainably.

Participants benefit both professionally and privately from increased self-perception and communication skills. Practical exercises ensure a sustainable learning success that is immediately applicable. The training also provides the basis for further NLP trainings such as Practitioner and Master.

Content of training

The NLP Diploma training provides central NLP techniques that can be trained in practice and applied directly in everyday life. Participants get to know methods to improve communication, achieve personal goals and control their behaviour in a targeted manner.

The main content at a glance:

  • Making a report: Techniques to quickly build confidence and shape relationships
  • Position of perception: Change of perspective on the objective assessment of situations and the promotion of empathy
  • Representation systems: Adaptation of communication to different channels of perception (visual, auditive, kinesthetic)
  • Metamodell der Sprache: Resolution of communication barriers through specific questions and precise speech patterns
  • Objective and resource activation: Clear definition of objectives and use of internal strengths to achieve goals
  • Submodalities: Change of inner images and feelings to promote new patterns of thought and behaviour
  • Time-Line work: Use of the internal time structure to edit memories and target planning
  • Feedback model: Constructive submission and reception of feedback
  • Positive language: Application of resource-oriented formulations to increase motivation
  • Basic principles of the NLP: Target orientation, sharpness and flexibility in thought and action
  • Introduction to the NLP history and the communication model: Understanding the origins and theoretical basis of the NLP
  • Anchor processes: Techniques for anchoring positive states and resources

These contents are taught by creative methods such as storytelling, visualization, role-playing and group exercises. This creates a living learning environment in which theory and practice are ideally combined. The practice-oriented approach promotes not only understanding, but also the direct implementation of the techniques in private and professional everyday life.

Duration

The NLP Diploma training includes:

  • At least 30 lessons
  • Distributed to 4 to 5 intense days
  • Available as a presence or online format

The flexible course structure allows participants to complete the training in a professional context. Online formats and presence courses are equally available, depending on your desire and time budget. The high intensity guarantees that central NLP techniques are effectively learned in a short time.

Methodology and didactics

The teaching concept is based on an experience-oriented and varied methodology. Theoretical knowledge is combined with practical exercises, which makes the learned directly tangible and applicable. Participants experience theory and practice in close interconnection – an approach that is not only understandable but also motivating.

Participants regularly report an "Aha effect" that makes the methods quickly understandable. The use of live demonstrations and group coaching makes learning particularly alive. Personal care by experienced NLP trainers ensures an individual learning experience. This creates a motivating learning environment in which individual progress becomes visible and experienceable.

Certification by INLPTA

This training is certified by the INLPTA – International NLP Trainers Association. The INLPTA is a globally active specialist association that has been certified by standard quality standards since 1993.

It aims to promote high-quality and responsible NLP training globally.

Certificates according to INLPTA standards are valid internationally and are entitled to further training for the NLP Practitioner and beyond.

The training at Landsiedel meets all the curriculum requirements of INLPTA and is carried out exclusively by certified trainers.

The awarded certificate is recognized worldwide and opens up access to further training levels such as the NLP Practitioner or Master Practitioner. The INLPTA stands for the quality, transparency and responsible application of NLP.

Continuing training according to the degree

After successful completion of the NLP Diploma training, the following ways of deepening are open:

  • NLP Practitioner: Extension of the basics and deepening of practical NLP techniques
  • NLP Master Practitioner: Learning advanced techniques for professional application in coaching, therapy or leadership
  • NLP Coach Training: Specialized training for the professional application of NLP in coaching

Each further qualification systematically builds on the diploma and deepens the knowledge step by step. Sometimes the Diploma training is also directly part of the NLP-Practitioner Training. Other specializations are also as NLP Trainer, Systemic coach or possible in hypnosis.

Benefits at a glance

The NLP Diploma training offers numerous sustainable benefits for your personal and professional development. Participants regularly report positive experiences and significant improvements in their communication and achievement.

  • Improved communication capability: Significantly more effective communication and positive relations
  • Increased conflict resolution competence: Constructive and harmonious conflict resolution
  • Clear objective and efficient achievement: Defining and successfully implementing goals
  • Personal growth: Detecting and consciously changing behaviour patterns, using strengths and developing
  • Increased professional opportunities: Developing professional skills and improving professional perspectives

Participants report more clarity in thinking, higher self-confidence and sovereign appearance. Many use the experience to improve relationships, professional success or personal well-being. The training often unfolds its potential far beyond the course period and has a positive effect in the long term.

The NLP Diploma training is the ideal first step for those who want to improve their communication, make their thinking more conscious and develop their potential in a targeted manner. Founded, practical and internationally certified by INLPTA – this training creates sustainable success in profession and private life.
