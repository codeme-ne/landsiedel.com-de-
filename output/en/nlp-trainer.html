NLP Trainer Training

The training as a NLP trainer according to the standards of the IN (International Association of NLP Institutes) is aimed at two target groups. On the one hand, the training provides the necessary skills and knowledge to carry out even NLP training. Your NLP knowledge is both theoretical and practical. On the other hand, training is interesting for participants who would like to make their seminars, lectures and workshops professionally with the help of NLP.

In this NLP training as a trainer it is about the development and growth of its own congruence and authenticity. It's about becoming a living example of what you teach. In addition, the aim is to apply NLP at the highest level, to expand its educational and communicative skills, to develop respect for themselves and other people and their model.

What does the NLP trainer teach me?




Content and topics of education

Seminar contents of NLP training











Work at the coach personality

In addition to high-level knowledge and action skills, this training for the NLP trainer is aimed, among other things, at working on key issues at the level of identity associated with the coaching activity:

  • What kind of coach do I want to be?
  • Congruence and authenticity
  • Visions and goals as coach
  • What does it mean to lead and teach me?
  • Self-management and self-coaching
  • Handling fears about training


Platform Skills and Group Dynamics

In the coaching situation, it is about being physically present and thus establishing contact with the group and reaching them with their own energy. In doing so, we help with techniques such as

  • Use of space anchors
  • Targeted use of gestures
  • Making energy contact from stage to audience
  • Parenting states in groups
  • Group Report
  • Group dynamic model by Michael Grinder
  • Handling of disturbances and objections
  • Covered anchoring with NLP formats


Training and exercise design

Here, the methodological and didactic seminar design is taken into account. There will be techniques like

  • The cycle of learning
  • Learning and memory
  • 4-Mat system
  • CPPP - Concept
  • McClelland's motivation model
  • Nested loops
  • The 3-R method (from Salgado)
  • Grinder presentation format
  • Preframing and Framing
  • Metaphors and Stories
  • Use of games
  • Chunking

and similar, as well as the sequence of learners and their translation in practical exercises. Efficient guidance of feedback and debriefing is also on the programme.



Coach marketing and profile

For a trainer it is not only important to make good seminars. He must also find clients and customers who engage him or register for his seminars.

  • Developing your own brand
  • Positioning on the market
  • Targeted use of concentration strategy
  • From Letter to Online Marketing
  • Trainer Networks and Social Media


Evaluation of trainings

Do seminars actually bring something to the customer? How can this be demonstrated? What can a trainer do to convince current and potential clients of himself and his performance?

  • Concepts for evaluating training
  • Model by Kirkpatrick
  • Three streets of learning
  • Influence factors on training and transfer success


Demonstrations before the group

Good demonstrations of NLP techniques are the heart of a good NLP training. It is therefore important to note some points.

  • Selection of the demon subject
  • Implementation of the Demo
  • Group involvement
  • Explanations on demo, etc.

Performance shots

Participants are recorded on video during their contributions. In addition to the detailed direct feedback on their contributions, they receive their presentations on video after training. Important areas are the congruence of the trainer, as well as the promotion of his individual training style, among other things the use of voice, gesture and body.




Certification according to international standards

Several recognised certificates

  • IN - International Association of NLP Institutes
  • LNLPT - Landsiedel NLP Training

We deliberately chose the International Association of NLP Institutes. It is the NLP association with the world's most members. It has been thought and traded for decades according to the highest quality and ethics standards. Our course fee includes the lifelong membership fee for our participants.

More about NLP Training

Created with NLP Training You need to train yourself in NLP. A NLP trainer should live NLP. He should be an authentic model for how to live and train with elegance and flexibility. His tools and methods allow him to inspire and motivate both himself and his fellow human beings. The NLP trainer training helps him develop into a congruent and authentic trainer.

Our NLP trainer training teaches effective techniques to inspire his audience as a teacher, trainer or presenter. The participants of the NLP trainer training learn how to organize brain-friendly seminars and make the seminar entertaining and exciting.

On the one hand, the NLP trainer training is aimed at those who want to offer NLP training even later and on the other hand to trainers who want to make their seminars even more entertaining and interesting with the help of NLP.

The participants of the NLP trainer training must demonstrate their competence both in practical and theoretical terms. During the NLP training, each coach candidate holds two presentations with about 20 minutes duration. For these presentations he receives an extensive feedback from the master trainer and a colleague. In a theoretical test, the trainer shows that he has extensive knowledge of the Practitioner, Master and Trainer material.
