What is NLP?

NLP simply explained

Neurolinguistic programming, in short NLP, is a model of motivation and communication developed in the 1970s. It has been developed by the analysis of very successful psychotherapists and is now used in a wide range of life areas, such as therapy, sales, leadership, partnership and dealing with children. Graves Value Model

Where we come from and where we go

The psychologist Clare Graves has developed a model that describes the development of man and his values. In this eBook I describe this model. Graves identified eight different value systems. He builds on the work of Maslow, which has become famous by his need pyramids.

This model lets you understand the life and history of humanity. Other things are at the forefront of each level in life. These have an influence on our form of society, dealing with our partners, meaning in life and much more. The model helps us to understand why wars break out and conflicts arise. It also helps us find ways to end this and find inner peace.

Those who use this model wisely will be able to convince more people and understand themselves much better. He'll be a better manager and a better partner. The model is very powerful.

Force

Seminar work "Manipulation through Neuro-Linguistic Programming?"

In the German Duden, manipulation is called “invincible, skillful action, with which someone obtains an advantage or gains something sought after”. The term can also be described as “consciently affecting the other”. - Rebekka Güthler

Is NLP manipulation?

In this e-book, Rebekka Güthler examined in the course of her seminar work the extent to which NLP is deliberately used in order to be aware of the or the interlocutors. or to draw a benefit from interlocutors. In order to illuminate this, various practical application examples are listed starting from a compressed conception of NLP. In addition, different opinions of NLP users on the manipulative force of NLP are analyzed. Various scenarios are listed that show how and in what areas NLP could be manipulating. It is my aim to consider objectively the controversial, repeatedly present discussion. In conclusion, various answers to the question are presented and a conclusion is drawn from the information collected.
