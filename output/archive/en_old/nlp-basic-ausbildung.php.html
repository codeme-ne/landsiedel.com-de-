NLP Basic Training: NLP-Basic - an NLP training after DVNLP with educational leave

What is neurolinguistic programming?

Neurolinguistic programming allows you to optimize your communication and develop your personality. NLP helps to understand itself better and to identify behaviour patterns. More precisely, it is about patterns in thought and behavior. These patterns affect our entire life. Coaches learn methods here to effectively support people. You experience effective communication, personal growth and mental self-control. Immediately experience how small changes in perception and expression can have a great effect – both professional and private. Learn about subtleties of nonverbal communication and many techniques of the NLP. The training is especially suitable for people who need to communicate professionally and want to immerse themselves in the world of neuro-linguistic programming. NLP deals with the question of how people experience, think, communicate and change. It explores how language influences our thinking, how convictions arise, how we store experiences – and how all this can be influenced in a targeted manner. NLP does not offer a rigid method, but rather a collection of effective models that can be flexibly adapted to the respective situation.

NLP-Basic-Ausbildung als Einstieg in die Welt des NLP  

This training offers you an entry into the fascinating world of the NLP. It is ideal for those who understand themselves better, get in touch with others more clearly and want to deal more consciously with language and internal processes. This practical basic training is about living access to neurolinguistic programming. Every NLP wants to learn, sooner or later, does not come around practical exercises. It is best to make these experiences in a great group and with an experienced trainer. All our trainers have years of experience and pass on their knowledge and skills with great pleasure to you. 

Whether as an impulse for personal development, to strengthen interpersonal skills or as an orientation for a later NLP-Practitioner training: The NLP basic training opens doors to more self-effectiveness, inner clarity, methods of change and authentic communication. The NLP training can later be continued to be a certified NLP practitioner. In some cases, educational leave is also possible for this training. 

Content – what you learn in basic education

This training provides central foundations of neurolinguistic programming in a compact and lively manner. The focus is on skills that promote the conscious handling of language, perception and internal processes – for more clarity, presence and effect in personal and professional everyday life.

Here is an overview of the central contents: 

1. Selected basic assumptions of the NLP

Two. Ethics Code of DVNLP

3. Calibration, Pacing and Leading

4. Produce and maintain report 5. Representation systems VAKOG

6. Perception positions (I-Association, Dissociation, Du-Association, Meta)

7. Objective

8. Basics of anchoring

9. Introduction to Logical Levels

10. Introduction to the meta-model of language

11. Valuation and Sensual Feedback

Certificate and membership of the DVNLP

After successful and complete participation in the NLP Basic training and the behavioral integration of the mediated content, the participants receive the official certificate “NLP Basic, DVNLP”. This confirms the acquired competences in dealing with communication, targeting, perception and internal resources. The certificate demonstrates compliance with the DVNLP guidelines and, in addition to the official DVNLP seal, also includes the total duration of training in days and hours, broken down by online and presence times. The start and end date of the training are also listed as well as the signature of the instructor or instructor responsible. With this certificate, the graduates also meet the requirements for membership in the DVNLP.

NLP-Basic Seminar and other certified NLP trainings

In the NLP there are a variety of training and training in the field of self-experience and coaching. The training is certified by the German Association for Neurolinguistic Programming. Participants will receive a certificate at the end. In five days, the procedures and formats are applied to concrete examples and concerns. The base contains at least 36 hours on five days.
In the NLP Basic training get You have a thorough overview of the central principles, models of thought and fields of application of the NLP. The focus is not on theoretical knowledge, but on experiencing, practicing and understanding through own doing. If you want to fully understand and shape communication, you will find a solid basis here – either as a personal development impulse or as a start for a longer training trip.

The seminar is suitable both as a stand-alone development impulse and as an entry course, which prepares for a further NLP practice training. Those who want to get to know NLP, improve their own communication and take the first step on a recognised training path will find the right framework in this training – with certified quality, personal accompaniment and an environment that allows development.

Difference to NLP Practitioner Training

The Practitioner is the first big step in the NLP. The training goes over at least 18 days with 130 hours. The days and hours of the basic seminar can be counted here. In basic training, participants learn basics. With the Practitioner training you then made the first major official step. 

Difference to NLP Training to Coach

Die Coach-Ausbildung ist eine deutlich umfangreichere Ausbildung. Sie beinhaltet in der Regel den Practitioner und im Falle das Master-Coaches auch eine NLP-Master-Ausbildung. Sie wird auf Wunsch auch nach dem deutschen Verband DVNLP zertifiziert.
