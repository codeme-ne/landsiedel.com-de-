NLP Practitioner Training

In training for the NLP Practitioner learn Know and apply the basics of neurolinguistic programming. This NLP training is ideal for people who want to develop both privately and professionally. Most participants use the NLP training as a NLP Practitioner especially to do something for themselves and their personality development. Many develop such a great enthusiasm that they later consider whether they do not even want to become a coach themselves. NLP can be used both privately and professionally excellently. In any case, you will develop your personality very strongly. Our NLP-Practitioner trainings are conducted by NLP-Master trainers from the International Association of NLP-Institutes. This is the world's largest NLP association. Stephan Landsiedel is president of the German Association. Content and topics of education

Confidentiality
A sharpened perception is a prerequisite for successful communication. Only who sees what triggers his words and body language can react accordingly and act according to needs. That is why at the beginning of this training program a series of perception exercises, which are trained in depth by the further seminar modules.
keywords: training your own perception during communication with others, increasing awareness of body language and voice qualities, differences between interpretation and sense-specific language, concept of calibrating, targeting and using body language, giving and receiving feedback


Pacing and Leading
Masters of communication meet a conversation partner in his experience of the world and manage him on this basis. Learn art through verbal and nonverbal communication deep trusting contact to establish and conduct the conversation.
Keywords: rapport production by the concept of mirroring, pacing mood, pacing of statements, pacing of body language, taking over leadership, interrupting the rapport, listening active


Enable resources
Our emotional states determine our lives. The ability to adapt your feelings to the needs of a situation helps Not only for more success but also for more joy of life. Who is beyond to a great extent has the ability to change the emotional states of other people, customers, employees and partners will be happy to visit.
Keywords: Use existing resources to put the interlocutor and your own team in positive conditions – Building a positive group atmosphere and making it accessible at any time – Changing your own physiology as an elegant method to change your own states – Dissolution of depression, blockades and stucco states in customer and employee talk – Implementation of the So-Tun-Als-Ob principle – Development of power over the body


Anchors
Improve everyday life with the knowledge and skills of our natural conditioning mechanisms. Learn how to produce beneficial emotional reactions in a targeted manner and to dissolve restrictive ones whenever you want.
Tags: anchors: theory, definition, procedure, special features, identification of existing anchors, anchors set, negative anchors dismantled and stack anchors, use of hidden anchors in sales and employee talk, exercise moment of excellence, anchor for speakers and seller


Metamodell der Sprache
Be aware of the magic of your language. It is not only the basis for clear understanding, but also for rapid, targeted change. Use this NLP technique to get one of the leading language tools our time. Become a master of spoken language.
Keywords: Four simple facts about our perception, perception filters, basic information processing processes, recovery of lost information, transformation model: crash course, language patterns of the meta model, eradication, generalization, distortion, application examples hearing and constructing themselves, what is happiness?, problem and goal determination, wish staircase, future pace, future pace


Representation system
Immerse yourself in the sense-specific world of your interlocutor to gain deeper understanding and understanding. This sensual consciousness opens to you unfamiliar levels of communication.
Keywords: Introduction to representation system, the different senses, language and sensory system, use of the knowledge of the representation system to deepen the rapport – with minimal indications (e.g. breathing, speech speed, tonality), how the customer or employee processes information and finds access to his inner resources, introduction to mini strategies, eye access instructions: explanation and application e.g. in sales talk, sales strategies for VAKRoll customers


Hypnotic language patterns
Open the door to you unconscious potential by knowing and applying the Milton model of language. Hypnotic language patterns allow you to access trance states, and thus to levels of consciousness that give you otherwise closed.
Keywords: prejudices and objections to hypnosis, Why to learn hypnosis?, Conceptual clarifications hypnosis, trance, history of hypnosis, goals Ericksons, hypnosis principles, first exercises, language patterns of the Milton model, hints for trance inductions and ending a trance, construction of metaphors, metaphors, exercises


Reframing
The ability to illuminate behavior or a situation from different perspectives makes our mind free and mobile. Exercise the ability to see meaning flexible and the choices to increase significantly.
Keywords: definition of reframing, types of reframing: context and meaning reframing, practice examples, joke trance, separation of intention and behavior, part model in the NLP, parts party, six-step reframing, negotiations between parts, immigrants treatment by means of reframing, agrrement frame


Submodalities:
Discover the programming language of subjective experience with this groundbreaking NLP technique. This high-profile tool allows you to quickly and specifically influence your emotional life.
Keywords: Verbal indicators of submodalities (SM), checklist of possible SM, exercises to the SM, working out critical SM, contrast method, sending resources, destroying inner images and sounds, Pralinee patterns: building a strong motivation for unloved things, changing beliefs, anxiety opponent, Swish technique, Phobie technique


Strategies
Behind human behavior are strategies that control and control it. The NLP has developed methods to learn and teach the strategies of experts (e.g. the Creativity Strategy Walt Disneys). can. In this way, for example, creativity, flexibility, motivation and decision-making processes can be optimized.
Keywords: What are strategies?, Walt-Disney Strategy, The TOTE Model, NLP Notation, Eliciting a Strategy, Conditions of Benefit of Strategies, Writing Strategy, Flexibility Strategy, Love Strategy, Utilization of Strategies, Design of a Strategy, Streamlining Strategies, New Design of a Strategy, Installing a Strategy, Strategies interrupt, Application Options


Position of perception
We are often caught in our own view of things. Learn to be flexible, look at yourself from the outside and gain completely new opportunities to deal with stress situations.
Keywords: Perception positions, position 1-2-3, interaction of perception positions, neurological levels, targeting by the neurological levels


Living Way Work
Use your timeline to make a systematic planning of your goals and gain clarity in the integration of your personal experiences in the past for your future.
Keywords: basics of timeline work, eliciting of timeline, different timeline types, spatial configuration of timeline, speech patterns, phrases, submodalities of time, removal of bumps and holes, design criteria, tempting future, time-line reframing, re-imprinting

What's the NLP Practitioner training?

The NLP-Practitioner course is the first of three stages in the formation of the NLP. In the Practitioner course, the basic theoretical knowledge and practical skills are taught to the methods.

Advantages of the Practitioner:

  • In der Ausbildung zum NLP Practitioner lernst Du die Kommunikation mit Dir selbst und anderen Menschen dramatisch zu verbessern und wie Du Dein Potential entfalten und nutzen kannst.
  • You will be taught learning and creativity strategies that help you develop in these areas.
  • Changing into energy-rich and self-confident conditions and overcoming blockages are also among the training topics.
  • NLP offers a variety of methods to make its feelings, as well as behavioral and thought patterns aware.

The goal is to teach you methods and strategies that can help you increase your quality of life.
