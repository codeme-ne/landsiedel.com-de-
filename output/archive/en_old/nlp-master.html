NLP Master Training

The NLP-Master-Practitioner builds on the NLP-Practitioner content. The focus is on integrative and situation-specific Application of learned formats. Prerequisite for certification Training is a completed NLP-Practitioner training. Our NLP Master trainings are conducted by highly qualified and long-term NLP master trainers. We offer the Master in several presence and in several online formats according to the high international quality standards the International Association of NLP Institutes.




Prerequisites for master training

A prerequisite for a later certification of the NLP Master training according to the guidelines of the International Association of NLP Institutes is a completed NLP Practitioner training according to the guidelines of this association. This means that the participant submits a copy of the Practitioner Certificate, which we have to meet the minimum requirements is tested. If the applicant can only provide certification by another NLP association, there is the possibility of post-certification (cost about 100 euros for seal fees), if the training has met the minimum standards. Two important standards are that training at least 18 days and at least 130 hours (pauses are calculated). A further criterion is: that the standard training contents of a prctitioner were covered.

If the minimum standards have not been met, there may be the possibility of to retrieve the missing days, hours and content for a fee.
However, if the Practitioner training has lasted only 10 days or less, simply missing more than half of the content. If you want a certification in this case, we strongly recommend a qualified NLP-Practitioner training once again to complete. In practice, too large gaps have been shown here, which then Level of master training considerably lower.

Some participants are not concerned with certification. You want without certification take part in training. This is possible if the participant assures us that he does not formal certification has sufficient NLP-Practitioner knowledge and skills. There are sometimes cases, e.g. when the NLP training is back so long that there are no associations yet and in the meantime has been regularly worked with clients... comes but not certified etc.

Please understand that we adhere to the quality requirements of the association in whose name we award the seals and create certificates. Our service team will be happy to inform you which There are solutions in your case.

Historical development

In the history of the NLP, the NLP-Practitioner training was first developed before the NLP-Master-Practitioner. ‘Practitioner’ means something like "the practitioner." A great deal has been done in these training programmes and trained. After completing this training program, participants were able to use basic techniques apply the NLP. Over time, more and more NLP techniques have been developed. The training has increased over 40 days and so they started to shift newer techniques into a second course. This new training program was called NLP-Master-Practitioner. NLP-Master-Practitioner is often abbreviated with NLP-Master. After successful completion of the NLP-Master-Practitioner, participants know all essential NLP techniques and receive the master certificate from the International Association of NLP Institutes (IN).

Testing methods and master thesis

In a test, both theoretical and practical knowledge are checked. Additional for the acquisition of the Master-Practitioner certificate the independent processing of a small topic necessary. The subject can be chosen freely or on a proposal from the trainer.

Diamond technique
This technique is suitable for therapeutic assistance, for Coaching as well as for consulting situations and for everyday life. It succeeds by changing the background design itself into which the problem and the respective Solution were embedded so far. The result are often amazingly fast changes, which are both profound and sustainable.
