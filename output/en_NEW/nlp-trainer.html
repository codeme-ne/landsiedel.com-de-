NLP Trainer Training

The NLP-trainer training according to the standards of the IN (International Association of NLP-Institutes) is aimed at two target groups. On the one hand, the necessary skills and knowledge are taught during the training in order to carry out NLP-trainings themselves. Your NLP-know-how is tested both theoretically and practically. On the other hand, the training is interesting for participants who would like to organise their seminars, lectures and workshops professionally with the help of NLP.

This NLP training as a trainer is about the development and growth of one's own congruence and authenticity. It is about becoming a living example of what you teach. In addition, it is about applying NLP at the highest level, expanding its pedagogical and communicative skills, developing respect for oneself and other people and their model.

What does the NLP Trainer Training bring me?




Subjects and topics of training

Seminar contents of the NLP trainer training











Work on the trainer personality

In addition to the high level of knowledge and action competence, this training as an NLP trainer is concerned, among other things, with dealing with the key issues at the identity level associated with coaching:

  • What kind of coach do I want to be?
  • Congruence and Authenticity
  • Visions and goals as a trainer
  • What does it mean for me to lead and teach?
  • Self-management and self-coaching
  • Dealing with anxieties regarding training


Platform Skills and Group Dynamics

In the trainer situation, it's about being physically present and thus building up contact with the group and achieving it with one's own energy. In doing so, techniques such as

  • Use of space anchors
  • Targeted use of gestures
  • Making energy contact from the stage to the audience
  • Eliciting states in groups
  • Group Rapport
  • Group Dynamic Model by Michael Grinder
  • Handling of faults and objections, etc.
  • Covered anchoring with NLP formats


Training and exercise design

Here the methodological and didactical seminar design is examined more closely. Techniques such as:

  • The cycle of learning
  • Learning and memory
  • 4-Mat system
  • CPPP concept
  • Motivation model of McClelland
  • Nested Loops
  • The 3-R method (by Salgado)
  • Grinder presentation format
  • Preframing and Framing
  • Metaphors and Stories
  • Use of games
  • Chunking

and similar, as well as the sequence of learners and their translation into practical exercises. The program also includes the efficient management of feedback and debriefing rounds.



Trainer marketing and profile

For a trainer it is not only important to make good seminars. He must also find clients and customers who engage him or register for his seminars.

  • Formation of the own brand
  • Positioning on the market
  • Targeted use of the concentration strategy
  • From letter to online marketing
  • Trainer networks and social media


Evaluation of training

Do seminars actually bring something to the customer? How can this be proven? What can a trainer do to convince current and potential clients of themselves and their performance?

  • Concepts for the evaluation of training
  • Model of Kirkpatrick
  • Three streets of learning
  • Influencing factors on training and transfer success


Demonstrations in front of the group

Good demonstrations of the NLP techniques are the heart of a good NLP training. Therefore, it is important to pay attention to some points.

  • Selection of the demo subject
  • Implementation of the demo
  • Involvement of the Group
  • Explanations of the demo, etc.

Performance Skills

The participants will be recorded on video during their contributions. In addition to the detailed direct feedback to their contributions, they will receive their presentations on video after the training. Important areas here are the congruence of the trainer, as well as the promotion of his individual training style, among other things the use of voice, gestures and body.




Certification according to international standards

Several recognised certificates

Upon successful completion of the NLP trainer training, we award two certificates in a beautiful certificate folder:

  • IN - International Association of NLP Institutes
  • LNLPT - Landsiedel NLP Training

We have deliberately chosen the International Association of NLP Institutes. It is the NLP association with the world's most members. It has been designed and traded for decades according to the highest standards of quality and ethics. Our course fee already includes the lifelong membership fee for our participants.

 

DVNLP certification
At an additional cost of 100 euros, the DVNLP (German Association for Neurolinguistic Programming) certificate can also be obtained during this training.

Frequently asked questions about trainer training

How many levels of training are there in the NLP?

In most associations there are in particular the levels NLP-Practitioner, NLP-Master, NLP-Trainer as well as NLP-Coach. This is the classic classification. Those who have completed the Master can also take part in an Advanced-Master-Training, the Master-Class or the Master-Circle.

Who is the target group for the NLP trainer training?

Some participants want to be involved in NLP training themselves later on. For them, training as a trainer is very important and later recognition as a trainer is very important.
However, most participants want to use NLP in their work as a specialist trainer. It doesn't matter whether they train time management, rhetoric, leadership, project management or anything else. NLP can be used in all seminars or workshops.



What is taught in the NLP trainer training?

The NLP trainer training is about teaching with NLP. The focus here is on the didactic structure of training and the seminar design. Further exciting content on the topic of learning types, group dynamics, presentation formats, storytelling and much more await you.
The online trainer offers a lot of freedom for this. In the NLP online trainer, everything that NLP trainings have to offer awaits you: small group exercises, demonstrations, individual exercises, reflections, group trances, interactive lectures, etc. Take advantage of this opportunity and be curious about the possibilities an online training offers.



Do I get a certificate?

Yes, after successful completion of the training, you will receive from us the certificate from our institute as well as the certificate of the International Association of NLP Institutes, the world's largest NLP association. For a fee of 100 euros, the certificate of the DVNLP (German Association for Neurolinguistic Programming) is also available.



Can I pay in installments?

For ourselves, the process is too complex, because we are not set up in a larger way, and this would mean that the rates are too high.
However, there is the possibility to run the registration via PayPal payment processing after consultation and PayPal offers a installment payment there. However, please inform yourself about the modalities and costs before you ask us to set this up for you.



Can I participate as a trainer without any experience?

Yes! We have experienced people who have never stood before a group and at the end have delivered two excellent presentations that have elicited standing ovations from the participants.



How does the NLP trainer test work?

In addition to an appearance worthy of an NLP trainer, you will need two passed presentations with a length of about 20 minutes for a certification. The NLP topics for this will be drawn out.
In addition, there is extensive written testing towards the end of the training.

Overview of training plan

The NLP Trainer Training is the third major stage in NLP training. This training is required for participants using NLP as a trainer or to train and train NLP themselves as an NLP trainer. For a certified degree, an NLP Master Training is a prerequisite. If you simply want to participate in improving your trainer skills, you don't have to prove a certificate. However, a high interest in NLP should be given. From our point of view, the NLP Trainer Training is one of the highest quality trainer trainings ever. The participants learn practical skills that can be used immediately, which immediately make the trainer everyday life very easier and inspire the participants!

Further information on NLP trainer training

With the NLP trainer training, you can create the prerequisite to train yourself in NLP. An NLP trainer should live NLP. He should be an authentic role model for how to live and train with elegance and flexibility. His tools and methods allow him to inspire and motivate both himself and his fellow human beings. The NLP trainer training helps him to develop into a congruent and authentic trainer.

Our NLP trainer training provides effective techniques to inspire your audience as a teacher, trainer or presenter. The participants of the NLP trainer training learn how to design brain-appropriate seminars and make the seminar entertaining and exciting.

On the one hand, the NLP trainer training is aimed at those who want to offer NLP training even later, and on the other hand at trainers who want to make their seminars even more entertaining and interesting with the help of NLP.

The participants of the NLP-trainer training must demonstrate their competence in both practical and theoretical terms. During the NLP-trainer training each trainer candidate holds two presentations with approx. 20 minutes duration. To these presentations he receives extensive feedback from the master trainer and a colleague. In a theoretical testing the trainer shows that he has extensive knowledge of the practitioner, master and trainer materials.
