What is NLP?

NLP simply explained

Neurolinguistic programming, NLP in short, is a motivational and communication model developed in the seventies. It was developed by the analysis of very successful psychotherapists and is now used in a wide variety of areas of life, such as therapy, sales, leadership, partnership and dealing with children. This e-book is an introduction to neurolinguistic programming.

Graves Value Model

Where we come from and where we go

The psychology teacher Clare Graves has developed a model that describes the development of man and his values. In this eBook I describe this model. Graves identified eight different value systems. He builds on the works of Maslow, who has become famous through his need pyramid.

This model allows us to understand our own life and the history of humanity. On every level, different things are in the foreground in life. These have an influence on our social form, our dealings with our partners, the meaning in life and much more. The model helps us to understand why wars break out and conflicts arise. It also helps us to find ways to end them and find inner peace.

Those who use this model wisely will be able to convince more people and understand themselves much better. They will be a better leader and a better partner. The model is very powerful.

Manipulation

Seminar paper "Manipulation by Neuro-Linguistic Programming?"

In the German Duden, manipulation is referred to as "untransparent, skillful action, with which someone acquires an advantage or gains something desired." The term can also be described as "knowingly taking influence on the other. - Rebekka Güthler

Is NLP manipulation?

In this e-book, Rebekka Güthler investigated the extent to which NLP is consciously used in order to benefit even those who are interlocutors or interlocutors. In order to illustrate this, various practical application examples are listed based on a compressed concept of NLP. In addition, different opinions of NLP users on the manipulative force of NLP are analyzed. Various scenarios are presented, which show how and in which areas NLP could manipulate. In this context, my goal is to objectively consider the controversial, constantly present discussion. Finally, various possible answers are shown to the question and a conclusion is drawn from the collected information.
